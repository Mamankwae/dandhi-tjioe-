Services

Perfecting Stroke

Perfecting Stroke or perfecting style in swimming is very important and influential in long distance swimming and sprinting.

DryLand

"DryLand" training or land training is very important for swimmers to increase stamina, endurance, strength, speed, and the other training.

Analyzing

Analyzing is very important for athletes to realize their own mistakes and important for coaches to make improvements for the future.

Motivation

All athletes or people must have experienced boredom, lazyness, despair, feeling like giving up and many more emotions that make someone sad/disappointed because of various things. So it is important for a coach to raise enthusiasm or motivation for athletes to continue to have the courage to move forward.

Nutrision

An athlete must maintain the nutrition he consumes every day. We as coaches must regulate the nutrition of the athletes we train. Both from sufficient protein, not excessive carbohydrates, and other things that help the athlete's body condition remain optimal and proportional for a swimmer.

Swim Meet

It is important for athletes to do Swim meets or simulate matches of other athletes. We as coaches must have connections with other coaches so that the athletes we train get many benefits such as experience, friends, tips and tricks and so on.
